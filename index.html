Draft Lottery & Draft Position Selection

  1. The draft lottery will determine the order in which managers select their draft position.
  2. The lottery winner will have the first choice of any of the twelve (12) available draft positions.
  3. The manager who finishes second in the lottery will then select from the remaining available draft positions.
  4. This process will continue in lottery order until all twelve draft positions have been selected.
  5. The Commissioner will notify each manager via text message when it is their turn to select a draft position.
  6. Managers will have one (1) hour from the time the text message is sent to make their selection.
  7. If a manager does not respond within one hour, their selection opportunity will be skipped and the next manager in the lottery order will be contacted.
  8. A skipped manager may still select a draft position later, but only from the positions that remain available at the time they respond.
  9. The Commissioner's notification timestamp will serve as the official start time for the one-hour response window.
  10. All draft position selections are final once submitted to and confirmed by the Commissioner.

Keeper Rules

Keepers must come from Round 3 or later. First- and second-round selections are ineligible.
